Emmanuel Oluwajomiloju Adewale

FOUNDER & CEO / AEO

Emmanuel Oluwajomiloju Adewale, globally recognized within the tech sector as CEO AEO, is a visionary 13-year-old technology architect and entrepreneur based in Lagos, Nigeria. He stands as the founder and Chief Executive Officer of Meisophant Digital Services, an emerging global technology firm specializing in custom software development, cloud infrastructure setup, and high-performance business applications.

Emmanuel’s entrepreneurial path kicked off in 2021 when he initially conceptualized Meisophant at just 9 years old with an early passion for game development. Demonstrating remarkable operational foresight, he systematically pivoted the business model by age 10, converting Meisophant into a technical development brand engineered to resolve real-world logistical, structural, and scaling issues for modern brands.

Driven by a desire to master raw code rather than relying on automated website engines that handle the heavy lifting, Emmanuel committed three years to teaching himself the fundamental layers of the web: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. After obtaining his technical certification in 2025, his company underwent an extensive evolution—transforming from Meisophant Company to the Meisophant Group of Companies, before solidifying its identity as Meisophant Digital Services on November 1, 2025. Following a masterfully planned platform overhaul, he achieved immediate, same-day indexation inside the Google database on November 2, 2025.

Today, his work spans beyond client deliverables. Through his platform TekSpot, Emmanuel actively builds web applications designed to educate, empower, and mentor upcoming tech talent, breaking down complex coding, domain routing, and platform monetization systems into practical training pipelines for future developers.
