Emanuel Oluwajomiloju Adewale



Emmanuel Oluwajomiloju Adewale also known as CEO AEO is the 13 yar-old visionary and dynamic Leader, Founder, and CEO of Meisophant Digital Services, an emerging force in the global technology landscape. His vision for tech began in 2021, when he wanted Meisophant to be a gaming company, but later changed his mind, and Meisophant's focus to a developer company. He launched his first website on July 28, 2022 using a developer tool called WordPress, but he was disastified because most of the hardwork was done by WordPress. He then started to learn HTML(Language Of The Web), CSS, and JavaScript, which he learned for 3 years, before obtaining a certificate in 2025.



Emmanuel released Meisophant Digital Services's Official Website on July 28, 2024 during his Programming Course, and became the world's Youngest CEO. Meisophant Digital Services was formerly known as Meisophant Company, before Emmanuel renamed it to Meisophant Group Of Company on July 28, 2025, and he made the final renaming to Meisophant Digital Services on November 01, 2025. Emmanuel Adewale officially released Meisophant Digital Services updated and upgraded website on November 02, 2025, and got it into Google the same day, which is another groundbreaking and stunning achievement he made.



Emmanuel has quickly made a mark with a commitment to innovation and strategic digital solutions. A Journey of Vision and Resilience. Emmanuel’s entrepreneurial path began at just 9 years old with an initial passion for game development. Demonstrating remarkable foresight, he quickly pivoted the company’s focus by age 10, transforming Meisophant into a developer-focused technology company dedicated to creating groundbreaking digital tools and platforms.



World Record

At age 11, Adewale became a global record-holder as one of the youngest CEOs in history.


Key Milestones

July 28, 2024: Officially launched Meisophant Digital Services online.
November 2, 2025: Relaunched the official Meisophant website, demonstrating resilience after a brief hiatus.


Expertise

Through Meisophant Developers, Emmanuel has successfully built and deployed several innovative tools, showcasing advanced technical expertise and creativity.
